About Us

Citra Selaras Logistik is a company engaged in the field of procurement of goods and services, transportation, general trading, and provision of integrated logistics solutions.

We stand to provide the best, fast, and precise service to all clients from government to private agencies. We are committed to being a reliable and professional partner.

Vision and mission

Vision

To become a superior, trusted and professional goods and services procurement and logistics company at the international level.

Mision

  • Providing the best service that is fast, precise and efficient.
  • Establishing long-term mutually beneficial partnerships.
  • Improving the competence and professionalism of human resources.
  • Maintaining integrity and quality in every service.
